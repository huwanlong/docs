## 安装

[https://developer.android.google.cn/studio?hl=zh-cn](https://developer.android.google.cn/studio?hl=zh-cn)

### 汉化

将`android studio`关闭，然后将`C:\software\Java\JetBrains\IntelliJ IDEA 2025.2\plugins\localization-zh`文件夹拷一份到`C:\Programs\Android\Android Studio\plugins\`中，打开`android studio`，`Files > Settings > Appearance & Behavior > System Settings > Language and Region`，将`Language`切成中文；
